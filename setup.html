Telegram Setup

Connect your Telegram bot and channels to manage subscribers and send promotional messages.

How to get your Bot Token:
  1. Open Telegram and search for @BotFather
  2. Send /newbot and follow the prompts
  3. Copy the token that looks like: 123456789:ABCdef...

The token @BotFather gave you when creating your bot

How to get Channel IDs:
  1. Add your bot as an admin to both channels
  2. For public channels: use the @username (e.g., @yourchannel)
  3. For private channels: forward a message from the channel to @userinfobot to get the ID (e.g., -1001234567890)

Your public/free tier channel where free users can join

Your private/premium channel for paying subscribers

Connect your Stripe account to accept payments and receive webhook notifications.

How to get your API Keys:
  1. Go to Stripe Dashboard > Developers > API Keys
  2. Copy your Secret key (click "Reveal test/live key")
  3. Copy your Publishable key (visible by default)

Starts with sk_live_ (production) or sk_test_ (testing)

Starts with pk_live_ (production) or pk_test_ (testing)

How to set up Webhooks:
  1. Go to Stripe Dashboard > Developers > Webhooks
  2. Click "Add endpoint" and enter your webhook URL:
    https://yourdomain.com/api/stripe/webhook
  3. Select events: checkout.session.completed, customer.subscription.updated, customer.subscription.deleted
  4. After creating, click the endpoint and copy the Signing secret (starts with whsec_)

Found on your webhook endpoint page - used to verify webhook events
