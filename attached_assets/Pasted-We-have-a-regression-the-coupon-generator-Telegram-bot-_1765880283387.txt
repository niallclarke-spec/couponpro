curl -i -X POST http://localhost:5000/api/telegram-webhook \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \

Response:
HTTP/1.0 403 Forbidden
{"error":"This feature is only available for EntryLab"}

THIS IS WRONG. Telegram webhooks must be unauthenticated and must never be blocked by Clerk or “EntryLab-only” checks.
